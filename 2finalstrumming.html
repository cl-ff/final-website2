Standard strumming

Strumming is the action of striking multiple strings in quick succession in order to perform a chord. This is usually done with a guitar pick or your thumb but some strumming techniques utilize other fingers.

Fingerpicking

Fingerpicking is when you make use of multiple fingers to pluck strings individually or multiple strings at a time. This technique often uses the thumb for the lower strings and the other fingers for the higher strings.
